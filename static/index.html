 The Essence of
                            Wolfclaw

                        

                            Wolfclaw is not just a chatbot. It is a Multi-Agent Orchestration Engine
                            designed for local-first autonomy.
                            Our philosophy is simple: Give the user an AI army that lives on their metal, respects their
                            privacy, and executes complex missions via visual logic.
                        

                    


                    

                        
                        

                            
 Master the
                                Workflow

                            

                                

                                    
1. Spawn Your Agents

                                    
Go to Manage
                                            Bots. Define their "Soul" (System Prompt). Each bot is a
                                        specialist. One for code, one for research, one for creative writing.

                                

                                

                                    
2. Connect the "Hands"

                                    
Use the Plugin
                                            Store to give agents tools like the calculator or web search.
                                        Without plugins, agents are just thinkers. With plugins, they are doers.

                                

                                

                                    
3. Orchestrate Missions

                                    
Use the War
                                            Room for complex tasks. Let a Manager agent delegate work to a team
                                        of sub-agents and synthesize the result.
