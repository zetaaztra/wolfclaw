                                Fleet Deployment: A to Z Guide
                            


                            

                                
                                

                                    
 1.
                                        Birth of a Bot

                                    
Create a personalized AI assistant with
                                        specific goals.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Navigate to Manage Bots in the sidebar.
    • 
                                        
  • Click New Bot. Give it a Name and choose a Model.
    • 
                                        
  • Define its System Prompt: e.g., "You are a professional
                                            accountant who likes emojis."
    • 
                                        
  • Hit Deploy and start chatting!
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 2.
                                        Remote Control (Telegram)

                                    
Control your bot army from your phone
                                        anywhere in the world.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Chat with @BotFather on Telegram to get a
                                            Token.
                                        
    • 
                                        
  • Go to Deploy Channels → Telegram Setup.
                                        
    • 
                                        
  • Select your Bot and paste the Token.
    • 
                                        
  • Start the Worker and send a message to your bot on
                                            Telegram!
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    

                                        3. Using AI Roles? (Templates)

                                    
Don't start from scratch—use our
                                        gallery of 50+ pre-built roles.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Visit the Template Gallery.
    • 
                                        
  • Browse categories: Business, Legal, Coding, Health, etc.
    • 
                                        
  • Click Clone Role to instantly create a bot with that expert
                                            level prompt.
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 4. The War
                                        Room (Swarm)

                                    
Orchestrate multiple agents to solve
                                        one massive problem.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Enter the War Room section.
    • 
                                        
  • Pick a Manager Bot (Choose a smart model like Claude 3.5 or
                                            Llama 405b).
    • 
                                        
  • Checkmark multiple Sub-Bots (Specialists).
    • 
                                        
  • Submit a task like "Analyze this business idea" and watch the bots
                                            collaborate!
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 5. Flows &
                                        Visual Plugins

                                    
Dynamic workflows that connect the
                                        internet and your PC.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Open Flow Studio to see the visual canvas.
    • 
                                        
  • Use Flow Templates to import common automations (Scraping,
                                            Emailing, Searching).
    • 
                                        
  • Plugins allow bots to "Search Google" or "Read a Website" seamlessly.
    • 
                                        
  • Connect blocks to build the perfect logic chain.
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 6. Watch & Learn
                                        (Macros)

                                    
Teach your AI to use your computer by
                                        just watching you.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Go to Automation Studio → Record Macro.
                                        
    • 
                                        
  • Perform your mouse and keyboard actions.
    • 
                                        
  • Click Analyze with Claude/Nvidia.
    • 
                                        
  • Wolfclaw will turn your recording into a Perfect Flow that
                                            can repeat the task forever.
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 7. The Knowledge
                                        Base (RAG)

                                    
Teach your bots using your own
                                        documents, PDFs, or websites.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Go to Knowledge Base and click Add
                                                Collection.
    • 
                                        
  • Upload files (PDF, TXT, DOCX) or provide a Website URL for scraping.
    • 
                                        
  • Wait for the Embedding Wolf to index your data.
    • 
                                        
  • In any Bot's settings, checkmark your Collection to give it instant memory!
                                        
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 8. Remote Command
                                        (SSH)

                                    
Bridge your AI directly into your
                                        remote servers or VPS.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Navigate to Remote Servers and click Add
                                                Server.
    • 
                                        
  • Enter your IP, Port, and SSH Credentials (saved in Secrets Vault).
    • 
                                        
  • Use the SSH Plugin in any Flow or Bot.
    • 
                                        
  • Deploy your bot to Telegram to control your servers via chat!
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 9. Universal
                                        Integrations

                                    
Connect Wolfclaw to the world's most
                                        popular apps.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Open the Integrations hub.
    • 
                                        
  • Select your preferred service (Google Docs, Slack, GitHub, etc.).
    • 
                                        
  • Paste your API Keys into the Secrets Vault.
    • 
                                        
  • Your bots can now read/write data to these apps automatically!
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 10. Automation Studio
                                    

                                    
Put your entire fleet on a schedule
                                        with Cron-like precision.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Go to Automations and click Schedule New
                                                Task.
    • 
                                        
  • Pick a Bot or a Flow to run.
    • 
                                        
  • Set a Cron Interval (e.g., "Every Monday at 9 AM").
    • 
                                        
  • Hit Activate to let Wolfclaw work while you sleep.
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 11. Secrets Vault
                                        (Iron Security)

                                    
The encrypted heart of Wolfclaw.
                                        Protect your identities.

                                    
    
                                        
  • All your API Keys and SSH passwords are encrypted with AES-256 locally.
    • 
                                        
  • Only you hold the **Master Key**. If you lose it, the data is gone forever.
                                        
    • 
                                        
  • Use the Vault for secure deployments to remote servers and 3rd party APIs.
                                        
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 12.
                                        Analytics (Watch the Fleet)

                                    
Monitor CPU usage, token efficiency,
                                        and response times.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Visit Analytics to see which bots are the most active and
                                            efficient.
    • 
                                        
  • Check Activity Logs for a step-by-step history of every
                                            tool and prompt.
    • 
                                        
  • Perfect for identifying and squashing logic bugs in your multi-agent flows.
                                        
    • 
                                    

                                


                                
                                

                                    
 13. Wallet &
                                        Logistics

                                    
Keeping the mission sustainable and
                                        within budget.

                                    
    
                                        
  • Monitor your **Nvidia NIM Credits** and API costs in real-time in the
                                            Wallet.
    • 
                                        
  • Use **Settings** to toggle **🧒 Simple Mode (ELI5)** for clearer answers.
                                        
    • 
                                        
  • Keep your fleet lean and powerful by decommissioning old workers.
    • 
                                    

                                

                            

                        


                        
                        

                            

                                
                                Master Tips from the Wolfclaw Team
                            

                            

                                

                                    
 Speeding up Your
                                        Fleet

                                    
Use **Nvidia
                                        NIM** for the absolute fastest response times. It feels like the AI is thinking
                                        ahead of you. If you are on a limited connection, try the **Mistral**
                                        models—they are incredibly efficient yet smart.

                                

                                

                                    
 Staying
                                        Secure

                                    
Don't want to
                                        use API keys? No problem. Download **Ollama**, start it, and Wolfclaw will
                                        automatically find it. Select `Llama 3.2` and enjoy 100% private, free AI that
                                        never leaves your machine.
