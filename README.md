## 🧩 The 90-Plugin Army: Why Plugins?
A common question is: *"Why do I need a plugin if the AI is smart?"* 

The answer is **Efficiency, Speed, and Precision.** While a "Generic AI" can try to figure things out, a **Wolfclaw Plugin** acts as a specialized "Tool Belt" for your bots.

### What Plugins Do for You:
1. **Reduce Mental Load (Tokens)**: Instead of the AI having to constantly "think" about how to format a CSV or audit a server, it calls a pre-built plugin. This makes the AI **faster** and **cheaper** (uses fewer tokens).
2. **Deterministic Results**: AI can hallucinate. Plugins cannot. When you use the `SQL_Builder` or `SSL_Checker` plugin, you get 100% accurate results every time.
3. **Bridge the Physical Gap**: Some plugins allow the AI to interact with software it couldn't normally see, like local CRM databases or specialized engineering software.

**The Army Tiers**: From **Home Users** (Tier 0) to **Security Researchers** (Tier 7), we have 90 pre-validated modules ready to be "slot-loaded" into your bots' brains.
